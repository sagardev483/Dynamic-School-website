Our Mission and Values

At Sunrise English Boarding School, we are dedicated to empowering students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the future. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive educational experience that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. We believe in a supportive learning environment that encourages creativity and innovation. Our core values include a commitment to holistic development, ensuring that every student is nurtured not just academically, but also socially and emotionally. Join us in our journey to inspire young minds and prepare them for a bright future.

Explore Our Exciting Extracurricular Activities

At Sunrise English Boarding School, we offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities that complement our academic programs. From sports and arts to various clubs, our activities are designed to foster personal growth, teamwork, and creativity. Join us to discover new passions and develop essential life skills.

Engage in Team Sports

Our sports programs encourage students to develop physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills. Join our soccer or volleyball teams and experience the thrill of competition.

Unleash Your Creativity

Our art programs provide students with the opportunity to express themselves through various mediums. Explore painting, drawing, and sculpture in a supportive environment.

Join Our Clubs

Our clubs offer a platform for students to explore interests in science, technology, and more. Participate in hands-on activities and expand your knowledge beyond the classroom.
